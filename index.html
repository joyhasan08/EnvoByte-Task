Frequently Asked Questions

01

What Is Included In Your Web Development Service?

Our web development service includes custom design, responsive development, content management system integration, basic SEO setup, contact forms, and 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer additional services like e-commerce functionality, custom features, and ongoing maintenance.

02

What Technologies Do You Use For Web Development?

We use modern technologies such as WordPress, HTML5, CSS3, Java Script, PHP, and MySQL to ensure your website is scalable, secure, and responsive.

03

What If I Need Ongoing Support After The Website Is Launched?

We offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. These packages include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. We can customize a maintenance plan based on your specific needs.

04

Can I Customize My Website According To My Business Needs?

We specialize in creating custom websites tailored to your specific business requirements. Our team works closely with you to understand your goals, brand identity, and target audience to create a website that perfectly represents your business and meets your functional needs.

05

Do You Offer Seo Services As Part Of Your Web Development?

Yes, we include basic SEO setup with all our web development projects, including proper meta tags, optimized site structure, and mobile responsiveness. We also offer comprehensive SEO services as separate packages, including keyword research, content optimization, link building, and ongoing SEO maintenance to improve your search engine rankings.
